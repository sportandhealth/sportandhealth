Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your account, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the Menu tab within the app.
  2. Locate the "Delete Account" option.
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions to submit your account deletion request.

You can also request account deletion by contacting our support team at support@sportandhealth.world

Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our data safety and privacy policy, please contact us at support@sportandhealth.world.
